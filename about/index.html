Hi, I'm Radu

I’m the CTO and co-founder of Fermyon, where I created the Spin project and I’m working on building a new generation of computing platforms using Wasm.

In the past I worked at Microsoft in the DeisLabs research and development team, where I was a core maintainer for several server-side WebAssembly and distributed systems projects like Krustlet, Draft, Brigade, or the Cloud Native Application Bundle project.

Sometimes I write about serious topics, and sometimes I write short notes about personal projects and experiments.
